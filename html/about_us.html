About Us

We are not your traditional tour company. We believe an experience in a new place should be about connection – deep connection. A worthy new experience should allow you to live out your personal values and fulfil your curiosity, on your terms.

At Splendour, every tour is unique and tailored just for you. Every effort has been taken to ensure our experiences are classified Covid-Safe by the latest research and regulations in New South Wales.

Our vehicles are sanitised between each experience, sanitiser is offered to every guest, masks and gloves are available upon request.

Where do you want to go, what do you want to experience? We’ve got plenty of inspiring ideas to take you off the beaten tourist path to expose you to a different side to Sydney – the Sydney we love.

Let us take care of every detail so that you can enjoy Sydney safely in style and luxury.
